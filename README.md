## 💡 Study Tips

- **Review Manual**: Read once for understanding, second time for reinforcement
- **QAE Database**: Focus on understanding explanations, not just memorizing answers
- **Online Course**: Follow the structured path, don't skip sections
- **Additional Resources**: Use to deepen understanding of practical concepts
