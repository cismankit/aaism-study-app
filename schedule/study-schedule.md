## 💡 Study Tips

### Effective Study Strategies
1. **Pomodoro Technique**: 25 min study, 5 min break
2. **Active Recall**: Test yourself after each section
3. **Spaced Repetition**: Review material at increasing intervals
4. **Teach Back**: Explain concepts as if teaching someone else

### Exam Day Preparation
- [ ] Schedule exam at least 2 weeks in advance
- [ ] Know the exam location/requirements
- [ ] Get good sleep the night before
- [ ] Review key concepts morning of exam
- [ ] Arrive early and stay calm
