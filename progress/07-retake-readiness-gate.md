# AAISM Retake Readiness Gate

> Objective: verify exam readiness with evidence before booking the retake.

---

## Readiness Standard

Book the exam only when both conditions are true:

1. Two final full timed mocks are completed under exam-like conditions.
2. Results show stable readiness at or above the target confidence range.

Target readiness: `470+` confidence equivalent and no major domain collapse.

---

## Pre-Filled Mock Schedule

### If using 30-day track
- Final Mock #1: Week 4, mid-week.
- Final Mock #2: Week 4, end of week (48-72h after Mock #1).
- Decision: end of week 4.

### If using 60-day track
- Final Mock #1: Week 7, mid-week.
- Final Mock #2: Week 8, mid-week.
- Decision: end of week 8.

---

## If NO-GO: 7-Day Recovery Sprint

- Day 1-2: focus on two highest-impact weak concepts.
- Day 3-4: mixed timed sets and review.
- Day 5: targeted domain drill.
- Day 6: full timed mock.
- Day 7: post-mortem and final decision.
